Glitched

For this project I focused on creating a series of collages and illustrations, some that took on interesting social topics, and some meant to evoke a specific feeling. Once I had prepared 30 pieces I took them into audacity to create a glitch effect. Then, I create an HTML code that allowed the viewer to cycle through each image as through they were at a gallery. This piece is meant to simmulate a gallery experience and allow the veiwer to interact with a multitude of pieces.
